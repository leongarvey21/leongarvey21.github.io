Overview

Midnight Munchies, the short film of two friends ordering pizza for the night. Patiently waiting, their hunger places them in a dark twist.

I was tasked for a college project to create a short film with no borders, I chose the route of bad comedy horrors that are guaranteed to give a laugh, but still keep everyone on the edge of their seat.

Created Animatic drawings using Adobe Photoshop and Premiere Pro to combine all frames. Film edited using Adobe Premiere Pro, Using non-copyright sounds from freesound.org for sound design.

Overview

Rentables is a movie collection app. Here you have the ability to make collections of your favourite movies, your least favourite movies and rate them along the way. The look and style is based on 80's/90's blockbuster.

Registering

Signing Up - Login

Once opening the website you are greeted with a signup or login page, it is as simple as clicking signup in the top right to easily create an account with ease. Logging in after registering a new account is as easy as just typing back in the info. You can also add a custom profile picture if wanted.

Home Page

The home page was styled to look retro. There are interactive buttons that link to other page's such as 'Learn More' which will deliver you to the about Page to learn more about the site. The color scheme is blue and yellow for the reasoning of keeping the aesthetic of the old blockbuster.

I created sections to space out all the information along with a website statistics section that reacts to collection's on the site created by the user.
